Fresh From Our Farm

Discover the variety and quality of our sustainably grown vegetables and ethically raised poultry

Farm-Fresh Excellence

Every morning, we harvest the freshest vegetables and care for our poultry with the same dedication our family has maintained for generations. Our produce reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the health of our community. From seed to table, we ensure that every item meets our highest standards.

Fresh Vegetables

Our vegetables are grown using sustainable farming practices, ensuring they're not only nutritious and delicious but also environmentally responsible. We follow natural growing cycles and use organic methods whenever possible.

Garden Peas

Sweet, tender peas bursting with flavor and nutrients. Perfect for fresh eating, cooking, or adding to your favorite dishes.

  • High in protein and fiber
  • Rich in vitamins A, C, and K
  • Naturally sweet and crunchy
  • Harvested at peak freshness
Peak Season: April - August

Spring Onions

Fresh, mild spring onions with crisp white bulbs and tender green tops. Essential for adding flavor to any meal.

  • Mild, sweet flavor
  • Both bulb and greens are edible
  • Rich in vitamins and antioxidants
  • Perfect for salads and cooking
Available: Year-round

Red Cabbage

Vibrant, nutrient-dense red cabbage with a crisp texture and slightly peppery flavor. Beautiful and healthy addition to any plate.

  • High in vitamin C and antioxidants
  • Natural purple color
  • Long shelf life
  • Versatile for cooking and salads
Peak Season: May - September

Fresh Lettuce

Crisp, garden-fresh lettuce varieties perfect for salads, sandwiches, and wraps. Grown with care for maximum freshness and flavor.

  • Multiple varieties available
  • Crisp texture and fresh taste
  • Low in calories, high in nutrients
  • Harvested daily for freshness
Available: Year-round

Mange Tout

also known as snow peas or sugar snap peas, are a delightful addition to any meal. They bring a crunchy texture and sweet flavour, making them a popular choice for a range of dishes from stir-fries to salads.

  • High in vitamin C and antioxidants
  • Contains Vitamin A
  • Supports Heart Health
  • Versatile for cooking and salads
Peak Season: May - September

Premium Poultry

Our broiler chickens are raised with the highest standards of care, providing them with spacious living conditions, quality feed, and regular health monitoring. The result is tender, flavorful meat that reflects our commitment to ethical farming.

🐔

Free-Range Broiler Chickens

Our chickens roam freely on pasture, resulting in healthier, more flavorful meat. Raised without antibiotics or growth hormones.

  • Free-range, pasture-raised
  • No antibiotics or hormones
  • Natural, healthy diet
  • Humanely raised and processed
Available: Year-round by order
