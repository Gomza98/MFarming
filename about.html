Our Story

Discover the vision and dedication behind Matlala Farming—where a passion for the land meets a drive for innovation and growth

Personal Roots, Growing Purpose

Matlala Farming was born from a personal vision—to create something meaningful that nourishes both the community and the land. Located in the green lands of Bronkhorstspruit, the land spans 21 hectares of fertile potential. What began as a modest plot in Gauteng is steadily growing into a promising agricultural venture, guided by values of sustainability, quality, and genuine care.

"My journey hasn't always been easy, but it has always been driven by love. Love for the earth that sustains us, love for the animals in our care, and love for the people who trust us to provide them with wholesome, nutritious food."

Drawing on my background in law and ICT, I have embraced both traditional and modern sustainable farming practices. This blend of innovation and respect for the land creates a harmony that benefits everyone—from the soil microorganisms to the families who enjoy our fresh produce

Today, Matlala Farming stands as a testament to what's possible when passion meets purpose. It’s not just about growing vegetables and raising chickens—it’s about building a personal legacy of resilience and positive impact for the community.
